What is E - Farmers Market?

We are facilitating the farmers to sell their products online using our application thereby potentially making millions worth of profit for them, creating a robust eco-system and increasing financial inclusion at the same time.

Industry Problems

Given the high number of middlemen and huge amounts of post-harvest loss, poor quality of goods are produced in the market for a very high price. Still, farmers don’t get a decent price of their hard work.

134bn

Post-harvest Losses

Pakistan lose annually on post harvest losses.

25pc

Horticultural Crop Loss

In 2014-15, at least 25pc losses of horticultural crops means 3.5m tonne are lost.

Mission

To create a robust Eco-system where farmers could have increased opportunities of growth.

Vision

A society which combines economic activity with community benefit, led by agricultural entrepreneurs.
